# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to creating a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent and communicate with clarity.
- Accept feedback gracefully and focus on shared outcomes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, doxxing, or sustained disruption.
- Any conduct that undermines contributor safety.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Please report concerns to: `conduct@murmur.example`.
