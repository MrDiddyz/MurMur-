# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities privately to `security@murmur.example`.

Include:

- A description of the issue and potential impact.
- Reproduction steps or proof-of-concept details.
- Suggested mitigation if available.

## Response targets

- Initial acknowledgment: within 2 business days.
- Triage and severity assessment: within 5 business days.
- Remediation timeline: communicated after triage.

## Disclosure

Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities until a fix is available and coordinated disclosure guidance has been provided.
