# MurMur Pricing

This pricing framework is designed for value-based enterprise conversations, aligning spend with decision quality, governance needs, and operational throughput.

## 1) Value Framing

MurMur pricing should be framed around business outcomes rather than model-call volume alone.

### Core value drivers

- **Cycle time reduction**: faster analysis-to-action for complex workflows.
- **Risk reduction**: policy checks and auditable paths reduce expensive operational mistakes.
- **Compounding intelligence**: persistent memory and reflection loops improve future outcomes.
- **Integration efficiency**: lower engineering overhead than assembling and maintaining a bespoke stack.

## 2) Packaging Principles

- Keep pricing simple enough for procurement review.
- Separate platform value from pass-through infrastructure consumption.
- Include governance and reliability features as tier differentiators.
- Avoid unlimited promises; define clear fair-use and overage mechanics.

## 3) Recommended Tiers

## Tier 1 — Foundation

**Target buyer:** early teams proving one or two production workflows.

**Includes:**

- Core orchestration and interface access.
- Baseline memory retention and execution logging.
- Standard connectors and template packs.
- Email/community support.

**Commercial model:** lower base platform fee + usage envelope.

## Tier 2 — Growth

**Target buyer:** multi-team organizations scaling across departments.

**Includes everything in Foundation, plus:**

- Higher workflow and concurrency limits.
- Advanced policy controls and approval gating.
- Extended retention windows and richer observability.
- Priority support and implementation advisory.

**Commercial model:** mid base platform fee + scaled usage + optional services.

## Tier 3 — Enterprise

**Target buyer:** regulated or mission-critical environments.

**Includes everything in Growth, plus:**

- SSO/SAML and enterprise identity controls.
- Environment isolation and stronger tenancy guarantees.
- Compliance-aligned controls and audit export.
- Dedicated success architecture and SLA-backed support.

**Commercial model:** custom annual contract, committed capacity, and governance package.

## 4) Optional Add-ons

- Dedicated deployment environments (single tenant).
- Industry-specific policy packs and decision templates.
- Advanced simulation libraries and domain calibration.
- Professional services for migration and operating model design.

## 5) Pricing Narrative for Sales

1. Start with workflow impact and risk profile.
2. Quantify avoided incidents and reduced cycle time.
3. Position tier selection on governance and scale requirements.
4. Keep infrastructure pass-through transparent.
5. Offer expansion path from Foundation to Enterprise as operating maturity grows.

## 6) Metrics to Tie to ROI

- Mean time from request to approved action.
- Decision reversal/error rate.
- Number of workflows running with policy gates.
- Analyst/operator hours saved per period.
- Time-to-onboard new teams or new workflow classes.
